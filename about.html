Our Story

A journey built on passion, vision and craftsmanship.

The Vision That Started It All

ZIKAYI Trading (Pty) Ltd was born from a simple belief: construction should be honest, reliable, and built to last.

Years ago, before the company even had a name, its founder stood on a dusty building site watching a small family home being transformed. The workers he observed weren’t just building a structure — they were building someone’s future.

That moment sparked a vision: a construction company that treats every project — big or small — with the same level of pride, precision, and personal responsibility.

Our Journey

2018 – The Foundation

ZIKAYI begins as a small independent contractor focused on brickwork and general building work.

2019 – The Expansion

Demand grows rapidly. We add plastering, roofing, and painting services.

2021 – The Breakthrough

ZIKAYI becomes known for consistent excellence and dependable craftsmanship.

2024 – The Modern Era

Now a fully registered company delivering premium construction solutions across residential, commercial and custom building sectors.
